Welcome to the Financial Literacy Learning page! Understanding basic financial concepts is essential for making informed decisions about your money. Let's start with a brief reading on the importance of budgeting.

Budgeting: Budgeting is the process of creating a plan to spend your money. It helps you prioritize your spending, track your expenses, and ensure that you have enough money for both needs and wants. Budgeting is a key tool for financial success and stability.
